# Novelle

**The ultimate Goodreads replacement — intelligent, social, and beautifully designed.**

Novelle is a modern reading platform that combines best-in-class book tracking, AI-powered reading companionship, rich social features, and deep analytics into a single experience. Free from retailer ownership, free from ads, and built entirely for readers.

---

## Why Novelle?

Goodreads (Amazon-owned) has been stagnant for over a decade. StoryGraph has great stats but no AI. Fable locks you into celebrity clubs. Hardcover has performance issues and genre bias. Literal is feature-limited.

**Novelle gives you everything in one place:**

- **AI Reading Companion** — Chat with an AI about any book, spoiler-free
- **Mood-Based Discovery** — Find books by how you feel, not just genre
- **Rich Analytics** — Heatmaps, streaks, genre breakdowns, reading speed
- **Real Book Club Tools** — Chat, polls, voting, scheduling — not forum threads
- **True Data Ownership** — Import from Goodreads/StoryGraph, export anytime
- **Quarter-Star Ratings** — Because 3.5 and 4 are not the same thing
- **Privacy First** — No ads, no data selling, granular controls

## Features

### MVP (Launch)
- User authentication (email + Google + Apple)
- Book search & discovery
- Book detail pages
- Reading shelves (WTR, CR, Finished, DNF, custom)
- Ratings (half-star) & reviews
- Reading progress tracking
- Reading goals (annual)
- Social feed & following
- Goodreads import
- Dark mode

### v2 (90-Day Target)
- Analytics dashboard & heatmap
- Reading streaks & advanced goals
- Book clubs with chat & polls
- Notes & highlights
- Achievements & badges
- Spoiler tags, review images
- Data export (CSV, PDF)
- Privacy controls

### v3+ (AI & Differentiators)
- AI reading companion
- AI book recommendations
- AI mood-based discovery
- AI spoiler-free explanations
- AI discussion questions & quizzes
- AI reading coach
- Author verification & Q&A
- Marketplace & price comparison
