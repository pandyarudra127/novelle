# Core Values

## 1. Reader First
Every decision starts with the reader. Not the publisher, not the retailer, not the advertiser — the person holding the book (or device). If it doesn't serve the reader, we don't build it.

## 2. Simplicity
Powerful does not mean complicated. Features should feel obvious. The interface should get out of the way. Reading is the main event; Novelle is just the companion.

## 3. Discovery
Great books change lives. We are committed to helping readers find books they will love — books they might never have found otherwise. Discovery is not an algorithm problem; it's a human problem with a technical solution.

## 4. Community
Reading can be solitary, but it doesn't have to be lonely. We build tools that connect readers meaningfully — through book clubs, discussions, shared shelves, and conversations — without the toxicity that plagues larger platforms.

## 5. Privacy
Your reading data is yours. We do not sell it, we do not exploit it, and we do not lock it in. You can export everything, anytime. No ads, no surveillance, no dark patterns.

## 6. Accessibility
Reading is for everyone. Novelle must be usable by people of all abilities, in all languages, on all devices. Accessibility is not a feature — it's a requirement.

## 7. Continuous Improvement
We ship early, listen hard, and iterate constantly. Novelle will never be "done" because readers' needs evolve. We will always be learning, always improving, and always raising the bar.

## 8. Independence
Novelle is not owned by a retailer, a tech giant, or a publisher. Our only obligation is to our users. This independence is the foundation of trust, and trust is the foundation of everything we build.
