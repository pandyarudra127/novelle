# Mission Statement

**To empower every reader to discover, track, and deepen their reading journey through intelligent tools, meaningful community, and beautiful design — while keeping your data yours, your privacy intact, and your love of reading at the center of everything we build.**

---

## Why This Mission Matters

Reading is one of the most transformative human activities. It builds empathy, knowledge, and imagination. Yet the tools we use to support reading have stagnated. Novelle's mission is to bring the reading experience into the modern era — not by treating books as products to sell, but by treating readers as people to empower.

Every feature, every design decision, and every line of code we write serves this mission: making reading more discoverable, more trackable, more social, and more intelligent — on the reader's terms.
